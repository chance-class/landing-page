When I turned 18, I aged out of the foster care system with no support. Then I found Forward Foster. They helped me find a place to live, enroll in college, and access counseling and mentoring services. They also connected me with other people who had similar experiences and understood what I was going through. Thanks to Forward Foster, I was able to make lasting connections, build my confidence and skills, and have hope for the future.
-Amber B.
